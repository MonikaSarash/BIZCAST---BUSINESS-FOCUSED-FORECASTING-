Workflow
How it works, step by step
1

Model Training

The Prophet model is pre-trained on your historical sales patterns, learning trends, seasonality, and holiday effects from your data.

2

Data Ingestion

Upload your Excel file containing date and sales columns. The Flask backend parses the data and prepares it for inference.

3

Forecast Generation

Prophet generates a 30-day forward forecast with daily granularity, computing the expected demand trajectory.

4

Dashboard & Export

Results are displayed in an interactive analytics dashboard and can be exported as a professional PDF report for stakeholders.
